FAQ
Common questions
Everything you need to know before booking.
Do you really come to my house?
Yes, 100%. We pick up your phone from your doorstep within 1–2 hours of booking. No need to visit any shop. After repair, we deliver it back to you — same address.
How long does a repair take?
Most repairs are done within 24 hours. Simple repairs like screen replacement or battery change are often done the same day. We'll give you an exact estimate on WhatsApp before confirming the booking.
What does the 3-month warranty cover?
If anything we repaired stops working within 3 months — screen goes black, battery drains fast again, charging port stops working — we fix it again for free. No questions asked.
How do you pay for old phones?
We pay on the spot — UPI, cash, whatever works for you. The price is confirmed after our technician physically inspects the phone. You're never forced to sell if you don't like the price.
Is my phone safe with you?
Absolutely. We work on your phone with full accountability. You can stay present during repair, or we take it to our trusted partner workshop and return it within 24 hours. Your data is never accessed.
Do you repair all phone brands?
Yes — Apple, Samsung, OnePlus, Xiaomi, Redmi, Vivo, Oppo, Realme, Motorola, Nothing and more. If your brand isn't listed, just message us on WhatsApp.
What areas do you cover?
We currently serve Dwarka Sectors 3 to 19 only. This helps us ensure fast pickup and delivery. Follow us on Instagram @phoneplus_in for expansion updates.
